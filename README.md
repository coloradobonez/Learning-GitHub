## Lists
Help break up information into readable bullet points:
- Point
  - Subpoint
    - subpoint

Break things down chronologically or step by step: 
1. Step One
    1. First
        1. subpoint
2. Step Two
    1. First
        1. subpoint
3. Step Three
    1. First
        1. subpoint
- [x] Don't forget task lists! 
- [ ] They can come in handy to get things done.
